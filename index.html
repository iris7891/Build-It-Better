What Are Engineering Tradeoffs?

Engineers make choices based on what the design needs to do. A material can be strong but heavy, light but weak, cheap but less sustainable, or flexible but not stiff.

The goal is not always to find the “best” material overall. The goal is to find the best material for a specific job.
